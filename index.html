About me

I completed my Ph.D in Computer Science at Louisiana State University. I work with Dr. Nash Mahmoud on software privacy and app store analysis at SEEL. My research interests include mobile app privacy, app store analysis, natural language processing, and requirement engineering.
My research is supported by the U.S. National Science Foundation (Award CNS 1951411) and LSU Economic Development Assistantships awards.
